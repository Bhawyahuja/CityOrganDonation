About the website

With the advancement of technology and availability of internet to the young and old we have tried to devise a website that will help people to have access to the organ resources at their hand. They can have access to the data regarding-the organ which is required by the user, the respective institute where the organ is available and how can people retrieve the same. Whenever someone is deceased, the respective institution will update the data on the website regarding which organs are available, then this website will inform the recipient or another medical institute accessing the website, who can retrieve the organs. This website focuses on providing a platform for availing organs for the people who are in need.
